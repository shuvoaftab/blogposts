## What is WordPress Maintenance?

**WordPress maintenance** involves the routine tasks required to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and optimized. These tasks help prevent downtime, boost SEO performance, and ensure a smooth experience for visitors.

---

## Why Is Maintenance So Important?

* **Security:** Outdated plugins and themes are prime targets for hackers.
* **Performance:** A bloated or buggy site turns visitors away.
* **SEO:** Google favors sites that are fast and error-free.
* **User Experience:** Broken links or missing images drive users crazy.

Neglecting maintenance can result in lost revenue, reputational damage, and technical headaches.

---

## When Should You Do It?

* **Weekly Tasks:** Quick checks and routine updates.
* **Monthly Tasks:** Deeper performance reviews and optimizations.

Bonus: Some tasks like backups and malware scans can be **automated daily**.

---

## Who Should Perform Maintenance?

* **Solo bloggers:** Use plugins or hire part-time help.
* **Agencies & developers:** Offer care plans to clients.
* **Business owners:** Delegate to your IT or marketing team.

If you can‚Äôt manage it all manually, consider using a **WordPress maintenance service** like GoWP, WP Buffs, or WPManageNinja.
